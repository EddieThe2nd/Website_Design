About Us

Fashion ,Jewellery and the Visual Arts in the 4IR

The Faculty of Arts,Design and Architecture has rich talent and high-quality creations showcasing the knowledge, skills, and values of our institution. FADA houses all the creative disciplines under one roof and our focus streams are Fashion Design , Jewellery Design and Visual Arts.

With this we maintain a strong focus on sustainability and relevance, and engage actively with the dynamism, creativity and diversity of Johannesburg and the rest of the world. FADA has expanded each of these fields beyond their traditional boundaries through internal and external collaborations!
